The Joint Road Forward - UGS
A propsal titled "The Joint Road Forward" was submitted to Indo Dutch Science Industry Collaboration to study the design of transportation infrastructure in Randstaad and Bangalore with Shell, IIITB and Delft University as partners.

Games for Awareness -UG
A proposal with TARU for development of a two part awareness game to train local council workers to identify and respond to effects of climate chage at the city level.


Bangalore's tryst with Integrating transport - PV
We are documenting integration of different modes of transport in Bangalore. The project aims to understand the gaps between planning and implemenration through a video case study of Yeshwanthpur in partnership with IIHS-Bangalore.

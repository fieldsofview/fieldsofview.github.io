Last month, we were thrilled to have two of our tools, E-QLT and Solemnly Resolve, shortlisted for the renowned MIT Solve challenge. From Challenges to Actionable Solutions

  Fields of View's Waste Management Research  

Fields of View's Landscape Study on Solid Waste Management (SWM) in India, now accessible on our website, sheds light on the intricate challenges of waste management. The study's stakeholder analysis revealed vital interconnections, highlighting significance of collaboration for effective solutions. The report also suggests pathways for philanthropic intervention beyond Swachh Bharat campaigns, focusing on bottom-up policy frameworks, innovative plastic credit systems, and supporting waste workers within the climate movement.

Furthermore, our another study into Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) for waste workers aimed to enhance their safety. We proposed technical and social recommendations, including improved PPE design and specialized equipment for different waste categories, ensuring safer working conditions.

Bridging Empathy and Policy

  Fields of View's Mental Health Initiatives  

Fields of View, in collaboration with the Centre of Mental Health Law and Policy (CMHLP), showcased an abridged version of the immersive game, State of Care, at the global Mindscapes Summit initiated by Wellcome Trust. Engaging policymakers, experts, and participants worldwide, the game provided a unique perspective into the challenges vulnerable populations encounter while navigating public mental health systems.

In May 2023, Fields of View and CMHLP organized a four-day mental health policy advocacy workshop for youth suicide prevention at ILS (Indian Law Society). Fellows from Delhi, Mumbai, and Pune converged, engaging with public policies and sharing mental health narratives. An upcoming October session will further empower youth advocates to address community-specific suicide prevention, guided by problem identification and advocacy plan development.

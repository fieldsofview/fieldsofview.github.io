Proposals and Projects

 U-  Urban Systems P-  Public Safety and Security G-  Games S-  Simulations V-  Visualisations

  • Information standards for disaster management - P,S
    In this project we propose to use a multi-agent system to aid decision makers by deriving standards for geo-spatial interoperability to allow them to respond to the disasters effectively. We are currently working with the Department of Science and Technology, Government of India for this project.
  • Unforget - U,P,V
    The goal of the project is to build a platform that documents the response of our systems to crimes against women. The platform does not focus on either the survivor or the criminal. We have developed the prototype for such system. We seek partners to use the platform, curate data and guide necessary changes.
  • FPS for safety training - G, P
    We submitted a proposal to Bangalore International Airport Limited to develop virtual games for safety training. The proposal aims at developing a virtual game to help train the personnel in safety procedures and to assess their continuous learning.
